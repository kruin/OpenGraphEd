Resize Mode

To change the currently active Graph Editor Window into resize mode click the Resize Button, or choose Resize from the Modes Menu.

Actions within the Graph Editor Window

In Resize Mode, pressing and dragging the mouse at any part of the bottom/left boundary of the green box within the currently active Graph Editor Window will cause the entire graph to be resized or scaled according to how far the mouse is dragged. The cursor will change when the mouse is over the bottom/right boundary of the green box. (Note: Resizing the graph to a very small size, and then a larger size can have some undesirable effects due to roundoff errors during scaling.)
